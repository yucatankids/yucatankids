About Us

About Yucatán Kids

Yucatán Kids AC is a registered nonprofit with Donatario status, enabling us to provide tax receipts (facturas) to donors. We work at Casa Otoch, a state-run home for orphans and at-risk children in Caucel, Yucatán, where we are the official provider of English classes, serving more than 200 students aged 3 to 18.

Through English instruction and educational and cultural activities, we promote the emotional and personal growth of orphans and disadvantaged children in Yucatán, equipping them with skills to secure jobs and achieve independence in adulthood.

Founded in 2015, our team has grown to include three paid part-time English teachers and more than 30 volunteers from around the world, representing Mexico and five other countries.

Our Impact

What We Do

As adolescents transition from orphanages and into their future lives, they may have limited education and job skills, which can make it difficult for them to find employment and support themselves financially.

We provide English learning programs through cultural enrichment that open up a world of educational and career opportunities for these children. The support we provide has life-changing and enriching effects for them as they transition into their adulthood.

Children who have access to quality English language education and cultural enrichment may be more likely to succeed in school, pursue higher education, and secure good jobs.

Our Work

Our Core Initiatives

  • High-quality English language programs

    These programs are the primary focus and are designed to empower children to succeed in their education and future careers

  • Building community partnerships

    We seek to build partnerships with community organizations, and other groups to expand the reach and impact of our programs

  • Providing social and emotional support

    We offer support and guidance on how to build social support network to help children cope with the challenges they may face as they transition into adulthood

  • Advocating for children's rights

    We work to raise awareness about the importance of education and cultural enrichment for children and advocate for policies and initiatives that support these goals
