What We Do

Early development

Primarily in English, focuses on teaching children ages 2-3. Includes reading story books, singing and speaking. Teaching preschool children handwriting, numbers, colors, and vocabulary.

Preschool

Teaching children from ages 4-5 handwriting, numbers, colors, and vocabulary. Reading stories in English to improve listening and comprehension.

English Classes

Taught by a contracted English teacher. Volunteers also attend serving as teacher's assistants.

Learning About Me

Preteen weekly discussion group to help build self esteem and optimism for their futures. The goal is to encourage them to support each other and cultivate a community.

Capoeira and Jazz

Lessons taught by our contracted instructors. Promotes healthy physical activity and an outlet for their individual expression.
