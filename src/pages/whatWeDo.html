What We Do

Early development

Our early development program focuses on teaching young children ages 2-3. We cover a variety of skills, primarily in English including reading storybooks, singing, speaking, and teaching basic fundamental concepts such as handwriting, numbers, colors, and vocabulary.

Preschool

In our preschool program, we work with children ages 4-5 to develop a variety of skills including handwriting, understanding numbers, learning colors, and expanding their vocabulary. We also include activities such as reading stories in English to improve listening and comprehension.

English Classes

Our English classes are taught by three highly skilled and professional Mexican English teachers. In addition to learning the English language, our classes aim to provide cultural enrichment and help children develop important skills such as critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication. These skills serve as a foundation for success in a variety of fields and can help children adapt to new environments and challenges as they navigate their future.

Supplementary Activities

Activities and outings we do with the children that allows them to use English in real world situations. The outings give the older children the opportunity to explore archeological sites, art museums, parks, ballet, and various performances.
