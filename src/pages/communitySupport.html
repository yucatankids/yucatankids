Donor Thank You

We would like to thank our generous donors. Thank you so much for your support!

Yucatan Kids AC is making a difference at the orphanage, Casa Otoch. We have been teaching English at Casa Otoch for 10 years, growing now to include three part time English teachers, and 30 volunteers. This year, our students took the Cambridge English Language Exam and achieved A-1 and A-2 levels.

Our program, 'English Beyond the Classroom' has been approved by the state and we are providing English classes for the more than 200 children who live at Casa Otoch. We believe that every child deserves to reach their full potential and have a path to a brighter future.
